Policy-Guard Compliance

Policy-as-Code Compliance Engine

DSPy Compliance Zero-Trust Web Demo

The Problem

Energy companies must comply with complex regulatory frameworks (OSHA, EPA, state regulations). Manual compliance reviews are time-consuming and error-prone. AI systems generating reports or recommendations need guardrails to ensure outputs don't violate regulatory constraints.

The Solution

Policy-Guard is an automated audit pipeline that validates AI system outputs against configurable regulatory rules defined as code. Using DSPy for structured prompting, the engine checks every output against "Zero-Trust" constraints before delivery.

Policies are defined in human-readable YAML, making it easy for compliance teams to maintain rules without engineering support.

Architecture

  • Policy Engine: YAML-based rule definitions
  • Validator: DSPy signatures for structured compliance checks
  • Guardrails: Pre/post processing filters for LLM outputs
  • Audit Trail: Complete logging for regulatory reporting
  • Web Demo: Interactive interface for testing policies

Example Policy

policy:
  name: "HSE Compliance Check"
  rules:
    - id: "OSHA-001"
      check: "No safety equipment bypassing"
      severity: "critical"
    - id: "EPA-042"
      check: "Emissions within permitted limits"
      severity: "high"
