Legal-Eagle Agent

Multi-Agent MSA Contract Review System

LangGraph Azure OpenAI Gemini Multi-Agent

The Problem

Master Service Agreements (MSAs) in the energy sector are complex legal documents that require extensive manual review. Legal teams spend weeks reviewing upstream lease agreements, often missing critical clauses that expose the company to liability or unfavorable terms.

The Solution

Legal-Eagle is a multi-agent agentic workflow built with LangGraph that autonomously audits MSA contracts. Multiple specialized agents analyze different aspects: clause extraction, risk assessment, compliance checking, and recommendation generation.

The system implements "Deep Think" reasoning chains for complex clause verification, ensuring human-in-the-loop approval for high-stakes decisions.

Architecture

  • Orchestrator: LangGraph state machine for agent coordination
  • Clause Extractor Agent: Identifies key terms, obligations, liabilities
  • Risk Assessor Agent: Scores clauses by potential exposure
  • Compliance Agent: Checks against internal policy templates
  • Summarizer Agent: Generates executive summary with recommendations
  • Deep Think: Chain-of-thought reasoning for complex clauses

Technologies

  • LangGraph / LangChain
  • Azure OpenAI (GPT-4)
  • Gemini Flash (for structured extraction)
  • Python (FastAPI)
  • Cosmos DB (state persistence)
  • Azure Functions
