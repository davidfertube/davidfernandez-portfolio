RL Supply Chain

Reinforcement Learning for Supply Chain Optimization

Stable Baselines3 Azure ML Gym Environment Adversarial Testing

The Problem

Supply chain logistics in energy operations involve complex decision-making under uncertainty. Traditional optimization fails to handle unexpected disruptions, demand spikes, and edge cases. Before deploying AI decision systems, companies need to stress-test business logic against adversarial scenarios.

The Solution

This project implements a custom OpenAI Gym environment simulating energy sector supply chain operations. Autonomous RL agents learn to optimize inventory, routing, and scheduling while adversarial agents stress-test the system to identify edge-case failures.

The dual-agent approach ensures production AI systems are robust before deployment, identifying 15+ critical failure modes in simulation.

Architecture

  • Environment: Custom OpenAI Gym with supply chain simulation
  • Optimizer Agent: PPO algorithm for decision optimization
  • Adversarial Agent: Stress-tests by creating worst-case scenarios
  • State Space: Inventory levels, demand forecasts, transit times
  • Action Space: Order quantities, routing decisions, scheduling
  • Training: Azure ML compute clusters for distributed training
